Integrated Health Home

What is Intergrated Health Home (IHH)?

Our Integrated Health Home (IHH) is a team of professionals, including family and peer support services, that work together. Our team provides whole-person, patient-centered, coordinated care for adults with a serious mental illness (SMI) and children with a serious emotional disturbance (SED). Our Care Coordinators help IHH members to meet individual goals related to their healthcare needs. Care Coordinators work with members to provide information and resources required to assist members with healthy living!

Where can I get this service?

Service is availabe in Page, Fermont and Montgomery counties.

Who is eligable to recieve this service?

Service is available to children and adults enrolled in Iowa Medicaid and residing in one of the counties listed above.
