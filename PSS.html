Peer Support Services

What are Peer Support Services?

Peer Support Services offer opportunities for learning and skill building in a peer-driven setting. Our Peer Support Services offer a dynamic mix of groups, activities, and community outings, facilitated by a certified Peer Support Specialist with lived recovery experience.


Where can I get this service?

Peer Support Services are currently offered in:

Clarinda (Tuesdays, 12:30pm-4pm)

Red Oak (Wednesdays, 12:30pm-4pm)

Shenandoah (Thursdays, 12:30pm-4pm)
