Supported Community Living

What is Supported Community Living (SCL)?

Like CSS, Supported Community Living also provides home and community-based support to individuals living with severe, persistent mental illness. Together with their assigned Supported Community Living Worker, clients in this program work to develop coping and life skills needed to participate more fully in their communities.!

Where can I get this service?

Page, Fremont, and Montgomery Counties.

Who is eligable to recieve this service?

This service is available to adults living in the counties listed above, with a history of psychiatric treatment that is more intensive than outpatient care, more than once in their lifetime, that requires assistance/additional supports to function in their community. Iowa Medicaid or regional funding is required for eligibility.
